## Introduction
This Dashboard webserver uses cookies and/or browser local storage for the reasons outlined below. This document expands on the storage and data topics described in the [Privacy Policy](./Privacy%20Policy.md).

- "Owner," "we" or "us" refers to the person(s) who operate this self-hosted deployment.
- "You" refers to the person reading this Cookie Policy or accessing this website.

This Cookie Policy applies to you whenever you access this self-hosted Dashboard deployment. It describes:

- What kinds of data we store in cookies and local storage.
- Why we store this data.
- How you can remove it.

If you do not wish for this website to store your data in cookies or local storage, then you may not use this website/service.

## 1. What kinds of data we store

### Authorization
If you are signed in, we hold authorization/session data so that we can show you the correct information and keep you signed in.

### Security
If you are signed in, we use session data to ensure you cannot access features you are not permitted to use. Where forms are submitted, anti-forgery (CSRF) protection may be used to prevent impersonation.

### User experience
We use data such as your preferred theme, color choices and language locale to improve your experience.

## 2. Why we store this data
Where practical, this deployment prefers to keep preference data on the client (in cookies or local storage) so that you can directly control it, rather than persisting it on the host machine.

## 3. How you can remove these cookies
You may remove this data at any time through your browser settings by clearing your cookies and local storage for this site. This will reset your stored preferences and sign you out. For security reasons, authorization/session data may also be invalidated when the website/service restarts.

## 4. Changes to this Cookie Policy
This Cookie Policy may be updated as situations change. Continued use of the website/service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
