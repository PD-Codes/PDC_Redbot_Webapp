# Privacy Policy

> **Note:** This document is a template for a self-hosted deployment. It is **not legal advice**. The owner of this deployment is responsible for reviewing and adapting it.

## Introduction
This Dashboard is a website that provides a friendly interface to control and modify how a Red-DiscordBot instance behaves in a Discord (discord.com) server.

- "Owner," "we" or "us" refers to the person(s) who operate this self-hosted deployment and who have access to the website/service files or to the Red-DiscordBot program/service files.
- "You" refers to the person reading this Privacy Policy or accessing this website.

This Privacy Policy applies to you whenever you access this self-hosted Dashboard deployment. It describes:

- What information is collected about you as a user when you browse this website.
- How this information is collected and used.
- How and where this information is stored, whether on your device or on a backend server.
- How you may request that your data be deleted.

If you do not wish for this website to collect, store or use your information as described in this Privacy Policy, you may not use this website/service.

## 1. What information we collect
The following may be collected, depending on how the owner has configured this deployment:

### Information you provide using the website/service
1. Display preferences such as theme and color choices.
2. Preferred language locale.

### Information from third-party services (via Discord OAuth)
1. Your unique Discord ID.
2. Your Discord profile avatar.
3. Your Discord username.
4. The Discord servers you share with the bot.

## 2. How this information is collected and used
We collect information to provide a usable and secure experience.

**Information you provide using the website/service**: Display preferences (such as theme and language locale) are used only to adapt the look and feel of the site to you. These preferences are not required, and you can choose how much you wish to set.

**Information provided by a third-party service**: This Dashboard uses Discord (discord.com) Open Authentication ("OAuth") to identify and authenticate you, so that your actions are limited to what you are permitted to do. When you are redirected to Discord's OAuth authorization page, Discord describes what information will be shared. If you cancel, no Discord data is collected. If you authorize, the data listed in Section 1 is requested.

**Information from the connected Red-DiscordBot**: This Dashboard queries a connected Red-DiscordBot instance to determine which Discord servers you share with the bot, in order to show the relevant pages. This data is loaded on demand when you visit the relevant page.

**Information your device provides**: For security purposes, this deployment may log technical request data (such as IP addresses) to detect suspicious activity. Whether and for how long such data is retained depends on how the owner has configured the deployment.

Display preferences and session data may be stored on your device through cookies and/or local storage. You can clear this at any time through your browser settings, though some data may be retained for the security of the service and other users.

## 3. Children under the Age of 13
This website/service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. If you are under the age of 13, you are not permitted to use this website/service.

## 4. Data deletion and disputes
If you have any complaints, concerns or issues with this Privacy Policy, or wish to request deletion of your data, you should contact the owner of this deployment.

## 5. Changes to this Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated as situations change. Continued use of the website/service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 6. Contact
For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the owner of this self-hosted deployment through the contact method they provide.
